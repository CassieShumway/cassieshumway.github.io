Project Footprints: Design Process

For the past few weeks I've been working on mock-ups for different projects. When starting out a design process, its important to sketch out your ideas, make mock-ups, and to keep all your drafts. The reason I emphasis that last point is because you want to see your design process evolve. To see the changes you made in order to problem solve and to keep ideas you might want to revisit later.

Presenting to a Client

Applicant Page

I presented this mock-up page to Ryan Verner for the applicant profile page. It had some interactive elements to it and I needed a clean and clear way of presenting those elements. In order to do this, you can create static pages that highlight each action you want to depict. In addition to presentation, gray out the rest of the page so the client can focus on what you want.
